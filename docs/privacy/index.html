Introduction

SeedPeers ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This policy describes how we handle information when you use our website and services (the "Services").

Information we collect

We may collect:

  • Account data — such as name, email, and organization when you register or contact us.
  • Usage data — such as pages visited, approximate location (derived from IP), device type, and timestamps.
  • Technical data — such as browser type, cookies, and similar technologies as described below.

How we use information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Services;
  • Communicate with you about updates, security, and support;
  • Analyze usage in aggregated or de-identified form;
  • Comply with law and protect rights, safety, and security.

Cookies and similar technologies

We and our partners may use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies for essential operation, preferences (such as theme), analytics, and marketing where permitted. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Sharing and subprocessors

We may share information with service providers who assist us (e.g. hosting, analytics, email delivery), subject to contracts that limit use to providing services on our behalf. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect users and the public.

Your rights and choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export personal data, or to object to certain processing. Contact us to exercise these rights. You may also unsubscribe from marketing emails using the link in those messages.

Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for the purposes above, unless a longer period is required by law.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the new version on this page and adjust the "Last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about this policy: use the contact options on our website or reach your account representative.
